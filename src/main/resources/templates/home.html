Classic Business Model

The Classic Business Model is a Java Spring Boot application developed to manage the operations of a product-based business. It handles entities such as Office, Employee, Customer, Product, Orders, and Payments to simulate real-world business workflows. Customers place orders for products, employees manage customer interactions, and payments are tracked after order processing.
